RULES OF THE GAME

Your goal is very simple , yet a challenging one.You will be asked to find the country name by simply watching the country Flag . What you have to do is to answer all questions. Correct answers gives the amount of points shown at the end. Incorrect answers shows you the red color. This means you lose the points of that question. At the end you shall see the User score calculated in percentage (%).


Safety

Be careful when chosing the answer. You will have 10 seconds to answer each question,that I think is more than enough. Even if you click the wrong answer, the right one will appear in Green after the score is saved.


Bonus Tip
